ABOUT US

We serve up authentic Kenyan dishes made with fresh, local ingredients. From Nyama Choma to Mukimo, experience the true taste of home. Karibu!

  • Mama Ramsey's Dishes is a restaurant located in Kwale that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of Kenya. We are passionate about using fresh, locally sourced ingredients to create authentic and delicious dishes. Our warm and inviting atmosphere makes us the perfect place to gather with family and friends for a memorable dining experience. Karibu!"
  • we believe that dining is more than just a meal – it's an experience. We strive to create a welcoming and comfortable atmosphere where guests can relax, connect, and enjoy the vibrant flavors of Kenya. Our menu showcases both classic Kenyan dishes and innovative creations, all prepared with the freshest ingredients and a touch of love. We are a locally owned and operated establishment that is passionate about supporting our community and sharing the flavors of Kenya with the world. We believe in creating a warm and welcoming space where everyone feels at home
  • Quality is our priority. From the carefully selected ingredients to the expertly crafted dishes, we are committed to providing our guests with an exceptional dining experience. Our team is passionate about showcasing the best of Kenyan cuisine and creating memorable moments for every guest
